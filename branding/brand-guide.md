## Logo Variants

| File | Usage |
|------|-------|
| `logo-icon.svg` | App icons, social media profile pics, favicons |
| `logo-horizontal.svg` | Primary logo — website header, documents, email signatures |
| `logo-horizontal-white.svg` | For dark backgrounds |
| `logo-horizontal-dark.svg` | High-contrast version for light backgrounds |
| `logo-stacked.svg` | Square layouts, social media posts, merchandise |
| `favicon.svg` | Browser tab icon |

### Logo Clear Space
Maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the truck icon around all sides of the logo. Do not place text, images, or other elements within this zone.

### Logo Misuse
- Do not stretch or distort the logo
- Do not change the logo colors outside of approved variants
- Do not place the logo on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast
- Do not rotate the logo

## Visual Elements

### Truck Icon
The green rounded-square truck icon is the core brand mark. It represents movement, reliability, and professionalism.

### Map Motif
The Europe map with Portugal highlighted can be used as a subtle background element in hero sections and marketing materials to convey the company's geographic reach.

---

## Tone of Voice
- **Professional** but approachable
- **Direct** — short sentences, clear value propositions
- **Reassuring** — emphasize reliability, care, and speed
- Use "123" as shorthand for simplicity: "1, 2, 3... cheguei!" (arrived!)
